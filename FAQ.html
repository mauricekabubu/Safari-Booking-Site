Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an adventure?

Visit the Trips page, choose your destination, and click “Book Now.” You’ll receive instant confirmation.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes, cancellations are allowed up to 48 hours before departure. Rescheduling is also available.

Do you offer group discounts?

Groups of 5+ enjoy special discounted rates. Contact us for custom packages.

What should I bring for a trip?

Comfortable clothing, water, sturdy shoes, and any items listed on the trip page.
